Graduate School Program
Graduate school is a postgraduate program of study that focuses on a certain academic area or professional field of interest. Although traditionally “academic” in nature (focused on the generation of original research in a specific topic), graduate school may also be “professional” in nature (oriented on the development of skills and knowledge for a certain vocation), or a combination of the two.

While the majority of undergraduate degrees allow students to study modules and classes of personal interest, a graduate degree allows for a considerably greater degree of flexibility. Attending extracurricular events and meetings, as well as hearing from guest speakers and lecturers as well as full-time faculty members who interest you, contributes to the diversity and multidisciplinarity of graduate school.
